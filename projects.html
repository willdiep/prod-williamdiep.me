    Stephanie Mankarios
    Mindfulness Yoga, Mediation, & Somatic Therapy

    Design, full stack development, SEO, & TLS 1.2 certificate

    Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 3, JavaScript, Nunjucks, NodeJS, KeystoneJS, MongoDB, Ubuntu, Digital Ocean, Let's Encrypt, & Mailchimp API

    Earned an A grade from SSL Labs

    Stephanie was looking for a design for her blog web app that would complement and highlight her yoga, self-healing, and body work services. Something clean and modern with a welcoming feel where her photos and content could take the spotlight. We continue to work together to keep the site current and fresh.

    Rather than implement a theme using Wordpress, I decided to design by scratch tailoring to her looks and needs. She needed a logo and was on a budget, so I decided to take an afternoon to learn Adobe Illustrator to create her She also wanted a platform to send out her weekly blog entries. Hence, I hooked up a Mailchimp API in the header and learned how to use Mailchimp to teach her. Lastly, I implemented Disqus to allow users to comment on her blog entries.

    Following the design, I developed the infrastructure using KeystoneJS, a NodeJS CMS platform, with MongoDB for the database. This platform uses a MVC architecture running on Ubuntu and deployed on Digital Ocean.
